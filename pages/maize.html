Grain Maize

About Maize

Maize (also known as corn) is a cereal grain domesticated in Mesoamerica and then spread throughout the Americas and the rest of the world. It's a staple food in many parts of the world and is used for a variety of purposes, including food, animal feed, and industrial products.

Karibu Groceries LTD sources high-quality maize from local farmers and our own farms in Maganjo and Matugga. We ensure that our maize meets the highest standards of quality and freshness.

Uses of Maize

  • Food: Maize is used to make a variety of food products, including cornmeal, tortillas, polenta, and corn syrup.
  • Animal Feed: Maize is a major source of feed for livestock.
  • Industrial Products: Maize is used to make a variety of industrial products, including starch, ethanol, and plastics.

Sourcing
